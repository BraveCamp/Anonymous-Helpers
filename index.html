Are You Being Bullied?

Bullied

Do you know what bullying is? Mabey not well, getting bullied is when someone hurts you physically, verbaly, or mentally. But sometimes people are just being mean the diffrence is that if someone calles you ugly they can do in a playful way or in a rude way and if some one tells you "Go kill yourselve"(Please do not try to selfharm yourselves it is not worth it or a good idea don't listen to the people that tell you this.) or something along thoses lines then they are bulling you and you should tell a trusted adult, no matter what they tell you to try and convince you to not tell an adult.

Cyber Bullied

What is Cyber bullying? Cyber Bullying is when someone tells you something that they wouldn't tell you to your face, like "Your post are bad. I hate you. Why am i your friend. Go kill your self(Do not do this) or something meaner" They are cyber bullying you and you need to get them off your social media fast.
